FAQs

What areas of law does Shazda Ford Firm specialize in?

At Shazda Ford Firm, we specialize in a wide range of legal services, including corporate law, family law, real estate transactions, personal injury claims, estate planning, and criminal defense. Our team is dedicated to providing strategic legal solutions tailored to your unique situation.

How do I schedule a consultation with your lawyers?

Scheduling a consultation is easy! You can call our office, fill out the contact form on our website, or send us an email. Our team will get back to you promptly to arrange a convenient time to discuss your case. We also offer virtual consultations for clients who prefer remote meetings.

How much does it cost to hire a lawyer at Shazda Ford Firm?

The cost of legal services depends on the complexity of your case and the type of legal assistance you need. We offer transparent pricing and flexible payment plans, including hourly rates, flat fees for certain services, and contingency fees for personal injury cases. During your consultation, we will discuss your options and provide a clear breakdown of costs.

How long will my case take to resolve?

The timeline for each case varies depending on its complexity, the legal process involved, and court schedules. Some cases can be resolved in a few weeks, while others may take months or longer. During your consultation, we will provide an estimated timeframe based on similar cases we’ve handled.

Can I get legal help even if I’m not sure I have a case?

Absolutely! If you’re unsure about your legal standing, the best thing to do is speak with an attorney. We offer initial consultations to assess your situation and advise you on the best course of action. Even if you don’t have a strong case, we can guide you on alternative solutions.
